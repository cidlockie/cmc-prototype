How mediation works

The mediation service will contact you and Jan Clark to arrange an appointment with a mediator.

You'll talk to the mediator one-on-one - you won't talk to Jan Clark directly. The mediator might need to speak to you more than once, but the process won't last more than an hour.

The mediator will ask you both to:

  • summarise the claim to help uncover underlying problems
  • consider the claim from both sides
  • be flexible

If mediation is successful you'll be asked to sign an agreement to formalise the settlement. If anyone breaks the terms of the agreement the case will go to court.
