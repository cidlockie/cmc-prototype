Continue using this service

If you don't want to respond and counterclaim on paper, you can either:

  • continue your response online with no counterclaim
  • continue online and pay the counterclaim fee yourself

If you continue your response online, you can't include the counterclaim, but you could make a new claim afterwards. This may mean you pay more in fees.
