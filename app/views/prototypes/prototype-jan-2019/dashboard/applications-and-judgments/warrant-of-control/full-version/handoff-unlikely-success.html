Consider a different enforcement option

You should only apply for a bailiff to visit the defendant if you think they'll be able to get your money back.

You should consider a different enforcement option. You can ask the court to:

  • order the defendant to attend a hearing and find out what they can afford to pay
  • take money from the defendant's wages to pay the debt
  • freeze money in the defendant's bank, building society or business account
  • charge the defendant's land or property

You need to use a paper form to apply for each of these options, complete it and return it to make your application.
