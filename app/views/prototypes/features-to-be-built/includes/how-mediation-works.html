How free mediation works

A mediator phones you

We'll contact you to arrange a phone call with a mediator from the Small Claims Mediation Service.

The call won't take longer than an hour.

You discuss the claim

The mediator will:

  • listen to each side separately - you wonâ€™t talk directly to the other side
  • ask you to consider what the other side says - they won't make you compromise
  • help you reach an agreement, if possible

After the phone call

If mediation is successful we'll ask you to sign a legal agreement that formalises what you agreed.

If either party breaks the terms, the other party can apply to the court to enforce them.

If mediation fails and a hearing is needed, any mediation discussions can't be mentioned in court.
