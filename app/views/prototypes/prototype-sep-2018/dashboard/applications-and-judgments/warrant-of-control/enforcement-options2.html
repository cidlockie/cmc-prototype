How do you want to try to recover your money?

You can ask the court to recover the debt for you if Mary Richards hasn't paid by the deadline.

You have to pay a fee for each type of enforcement. You can apply for as many as you think might get you your money.

I don't know much about the defendant's financial situation

You can ask the court to order the defendant to attend a hearing and find out what they can afford to pay.

How to apply: Download form N316 (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

The application costs £110. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you.

Where to send paper forms
County Court Money Claims Centre
PO Box 527
Salford
M5 0BY

I believe the defendant has the money, or items to sell at auction

You can ask the court to send bailiffs to collect the money. You'll need to apply for either a warrant of control or a writ of control.

Apply for a warrant of control

You can only apply for a warrant of control for a debt of less than £5,000 (including fee).

The application costs £77. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you.

Apply online

Apply for a writ of control

You can only apply for a writ of control if the debt is over £600.

How to apply: Download form N337 (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

The application costs £66. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you. You'll have to pay an extra fee of £75 if enforcement is not successful.

Where to send paper forms
County Court Money Claims Centre
PO Box 527
Salford
M5 0BY

I believe the defendant is in paid employment

You can ask the court to take money from the debtor’s wages to pay the debt. They do this by sending an order to the debtor’s employer.

You should choose a different option if you know Mary Richards is self-employed or unemployed.

How to apply: Download form N337 (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

The application costs £110. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you.

Where to send paper forms
County Court Money Claims Centre
PO Box 527
Salford
M5 0BY

I believe the defendant has a bank account

You can ask the court to freeze money in the defendant's bank, building society or business account. The court will decide if money from the account can be used to pay the debt.

How to apply: Download form N349 (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

The application costs £110. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you.

Where to send paper forms
County Court Money Claims Centre
PO Box 527
Salford
M5 0BY

I believe the defendant has property or land

You can ask the court to charge the defendant's land or property. If the land or property is sold, the debtor must pay this charge before they get their money.

How to apply: Download form N379 (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

The application costs £110. The court adds this fee to the total debt owed to you.

Where to send paper forms
County Court Money Claims Centre
PO Box 527
Salford
M5 0BY
