Make a money claim online

You can make a claim against an individual or company who:

  • owes you money and won't pay it back
  • is in a dispute with you - for example, you've paid for goods that weren't satisfactory

It could count against you in court if you don't try to sort out a dispute directly before you make an official claim.

What you'll need

You'll need:

  • the current or last known address of the person or company you’re claiming against
  • a credit or debit card to pay the claim fee
  • your email address so you can save your claim and get updates

You might be able to get help with fees if you're on a low income or get certain benefits. Apply for this before you make your claim.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

A money claim might not be your only or best option. Find out about other ways to get your money.

Find out more about making a money claim, such as the fees you’ll pay.

Legal advice

You don’t have to use a lawyer to make a claim but some people choose to. For example, you might need to check if your claim is valid, who to claim against, or what to say in your claim.

You might be able to get free legal advice from Citizens Advice or Advicenow.

You can also apply by post.

You can print and fill in a paper claim form. Send it and a cheque for your claim fee to the the address on the form.
