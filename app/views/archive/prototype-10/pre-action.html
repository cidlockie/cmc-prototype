Before you make a money claim

You should try to solve the dispute directly with the party before making a claim - follow these steps:

  • Speak to them (send an email or letter if you want a record).
  • Tell them what you believe they owe you and why.
  • Explain that if you can't reach an agreement (eg a timetable for paying the money) you'll make a legal money claim.
  • Tell them that they might have to pay the full amount as well as fees and other costs if they ‘defend the claim’, and that they could get a County Court Judgment (CCJ).

It might count against you in court if you don’t try to sort out the dispute with the person directly, before you make an official claim.
