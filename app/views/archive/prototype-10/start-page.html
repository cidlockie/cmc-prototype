Make a money claim

Use this service to make a legal claim for money you believe you’re owed by a person or business.

For example, you may have:

  • paid for goods or services that were faulty or poorly delivered
  • paid for goods or services that were never delivered
  • given money to someone who hasn’t paid you back
  • done work for someone who hasn’t paid you

To use this service you will need:

  • a debit or credit card to pay the fee (check the cost of the fee)
  • an email address to save your claim and get updates
  • a current or last known address for the party you’re claiming against - the address must be in England or Wales

You can only use this service for claims of less than £10,000.
