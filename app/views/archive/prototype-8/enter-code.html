What ways can I respond?

If you accept that the claim is fair you can respond to say that you agree to pay the money. If you can't afford to pay it all at once you can make a suggestion, such as a repayment plan, for them to consider.

If you don't accept that you owe the money (or all the money that's been claimed) you'll be given options for discussing the claim, both with the claimant and with a mediator.

You can also make a claim against them (‘counterclaim’) if you think they owe you money. You might have to pay a court fee.
