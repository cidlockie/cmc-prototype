Other questions

What ways can I respond?

If you accept that the claim is fair you can respond to say that you agree to pay the money. If you can't afford to pay it all at once you can make a suggestion, such as a repayment plan, for them to consider.

If you don't accept that you owe the money (or all the money that's been claimed) you'll be given options for discussing the claim, both with the claimant and with a mediator. If you can't or don't want to resolve the dispute (by making an agreement, for example) you'll need to give the court more information to explain why you don't owe the money. A judge will then make a decision about the claim if you do have to go to a hearing.

What if I've already paid the money?

You might have to pay them back the claim fee as well.

Can I just pay the money now?
