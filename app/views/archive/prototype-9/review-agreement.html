Claim reference: CF91-BK65

Claimant: Jan Clark

Defendant: Mary Richards


You agreed the following:

What
Mary Richards will organise for the roof to be repaired on or before 1 May.
Amount
Jan Clark will pay Mary Richards £50 to cover the materials.
Payment
By cash or Bacs


Terms

  1. This agreement replaces any earlier written or verbal agreements you have made.
  2. The court won’t do anything more about this claim once this agreement is signed by those named. This is known as ‘a stay’.
  3. If the court hasn’t heard from you or anyone else named in this agreement by 1 August 2017 the claim and any related defence will be cancelled (‘struck out’).
  4. Don’t tell people about this agreement once you’ve signed it. You must keep this information confidential.

If this agreement is broken

If the other party breaks this agreement you can ask the court to:

  • enter judgment for you
  • reopen the claim for a hearing for the full amount claimed
You'll need to complete N244 General Form of Application.

The other party can also apply to the court if you break this agreement.

Statement of truth

By signing this agreement you confirm that any information you have given is true, to the best of your knowledge.

You also confirm that you have read and accept this agreement and its terms.
