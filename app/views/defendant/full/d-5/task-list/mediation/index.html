Free mediation

Many people resolve their dispute without a hearing by taking part in telephone mediation.

A mediator will:

  • listen to each side separately - you won’t talk to directly to the other side
  • ask you to consider what the other side says - they won’t force you to settle
  • help you reach an agreement, if possible
