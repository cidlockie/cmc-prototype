Settlement agreements

A settlement agreement is a legal agreement between you and the claimant describing how and when you will repay them.

If the claimant chooses to sign your agreement, the claim will be put on hold and they will not be able to issue a CCJ against you unless you break the agreement.

If they do not sign the settlement agreement, they are still able to issue a CCJ against you.

Do you wish to sign a settlement agreement?

If you do not make a settlement agreement, the claimant will be able to issue a CCJ against you which will harm your credit rating.
