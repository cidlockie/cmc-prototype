Make a counterclaim

You can make a counterclaim if you believe the claimant Jan Clark owes you money.

Your counterclaim will be sent to Jan Clark. They have 14 days to respond. If they reject it the case will be reviewed by a judge and may go to a hearing.

How to pay the fee

You'll need to pay a court fee if you make a counterclaim. You'll need a debit or credit card.
